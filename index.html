Our Story

Glen Haven Maple Syrup is the realization of untapped potential in the maple forests contained on Glen Haven Farm. Glen Haven Farm is a centenary family farm located in Ossian, New York. Michael DeBerardinis is a member of the fourth generation, and he has operated the farm for the past 30 years. Christopher and Nicholas are Michael’s sons, and belong to the fifth generation of the family to produce agricultural products from the land. Bringing new ideas and a lot of potential to the farm’s existing ecosystem, the fifth generation is ready to jump into the world of maple syrup production. Maple syrup will be our first crop of the year and allows us to produce during a traditionally slow period. The expansion into maple syrup allows for diversification on our family farm.
